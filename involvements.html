NHRI Leadership Mentoring

NHRI is a leadership development program that matches an undergraduate student with a high-school/middle-school student in the area. Coordinates with the ALEC102 Interpersonal Skills class to grow skills such as Listening, Trust, Empathy, and Strengths. 7 hr/week time commitment.

Pi Kappa Alpha Fraternity

IFC Fraternity at UNL that consistiently ranks in the top 10% of national chapters in terms of scholarship, involvement, service, and philanthropy. I'm currently in the process of running for chapter Secretary, and IFC Risk Management chair. 5 hr/week time commitment.

UNL Honors Afterschool Club

Self-organized and led afterschool club for elementary students at a local school. Our group meets weekly throughout specific semesters. Involves purchasing supplies and formulating lesson plans, as well as working with young children and coordinating with their instructor. 3 hr/week time commitment.

Intramural Sports Teams

I play on a variety of intramural sports teams for my fraternity and the Raikes School, especially indoor/outdoor volleyball and basketball. 3 hr/week time commitment.

Kauffman Resident's Association

Organization of students from the Raikes School residence hall, Kauffman, which oversees a yearly budget. Our main activities include hosting events and purchasing items for communal use around the building. 2 hr/week time commitment.
